Mercedes Amg Petronas Formula 1 Team

Mercedes-Benz, a brand of the Mercedes-Benz Group, has been involved in Formula One as both team owner and engine manufacturer for various periods since 1954. The Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Re-Entered in 2010 with Michael Schumacher - The Seven-Time World Champion, and Nico Rosberg - 2016 World Champion, son of Keke Rosberg former world champion. Lewis Hamilton Joined Mercedes in 2013 and went on to win 2014 and 2015 World Drivers championship, helpiing Mercedes win Constructors championship along with the contribution of Nico Rosberg. Nico Rosberg went on to win the 2016 WDC, with Hamilton coming second in the championship. Hamilton continued his and his teams win streak of becoming a 7-time World Champion equalling Michael Schumachers Record of World Drivers championship and Mercedes winning 8 constructors championships in a row.
